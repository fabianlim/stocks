Below shows a Principle Component Analysis (PCA) of SG Index stock quotes pulled from Yahoo. PCA computed for quotes pulled from the past 30 days.

Left graph plots std-deviation (i.e., singular value) of each component. Vertical axis given in log-scale. Click on dot to view features of component on the right graph.

Right graph is a radial bar chart that shows component features. Features comprise mostly of fundementals. Blue color means positive values, red means negative values. Components are normalized, bar chart range is simply [0, 1].
